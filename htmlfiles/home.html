About us

Virgil Abloh founded Off-White™ in 2013 as a multi-platform creative endeavor, a space for his continuous experimentation.
The main medium being fashion yet he combined ideas of streetwear, luxury, art, music, and architecture.

What offwhite represents : Off-white symbolizes peace, elegance, and fresh starts. It also shares specific associations with pure white, such as goodness,
spirituality, and innocence. In color psychology, off-white evokes a sense of comfort, delicacy, and refinement.
